About me

Hello! My name is Karolina Seidlova and I come from the Czech republic. For my first name is a bit long for everyday use in written communication (and spoken as well) so I use a short form of my name that is Kaja. And I decided to put it in my logo because it's short and it's part of me.

My way to web development was not straight and easy. Many people start their IT carrier almost at nursery school. However, my interests were different in the past. As a small girl I dreamt of becoming an astronaut discovering new worlds like in Star Trek series - that is why my favourite motto is "per aspera ad astra" what means in English "through difficulties to the stars". I still love star-gazing at nights and every unusual astronomical event is a great opportunity for me to learn something new about the space.

What I do when I'm not coding?

Not only mysteries of the space are my hobby. I love reading, especially fantasy genre is my cup of tea. Right now, I'm discovering amazing world called "Discworld" and I'm really enjoying it! When talking about discovering I must mention my passion for genealogy - shortly looking for ancestor in old archives written in old writtings. The last but not least is my love for music - I am a big fan of Queen and Adam Lambert, but there are many more groups I like.

How did I get to coding?

Firstly I have to return to my study experiences and expectations. Later I tended to be a chemist or a biologist and later I graduated from Tomas Bata University. I was fresh from crisis management and popuplation protection. Despite the fact that today's world is full of crisis there was no job for me. I was lucky and on the last November I was accepted for the first phase of the Udacity scholarship by Google and on the February 2018 I was accepted for the second phase. Yay! Now, I study hard to become a font-end web developer. Welcome to my first page that introduces me and my portfolio work!
